# Title CSS

## Font Family and Size

````
font-family: 'Marck Script', cursive;
font-size: 72px;
````

## Gradient

````
background: #EDECE8;
background: -webkit-radial-gradient(circle farthest-corner at center center, #EDECE8 40%, #6F5747 100%);
background: -moz-radial-gradient(circle farthest-corner at center center, #EDECE8 40%, #6F5747 100%);
background: radial-gradient(circle farthest-corner at center center, #EDECE8 40%, #6F5747 100%);
-webkit-background-clip: text;
-webkit-text-fill-color: transparent;
````

## Shadow

````
filter: drop-shadow(2px 2px #6F5747);
````
